About Us

Our mission is to make free and open source Bible software for the world. Our software is not made to restrict you from sharing God's word or even the app itself. Their sole purpose is to help anyone freely read the Bible. All of the Heb12 software are all available for anyone to freely use, modify, and share with others. There is no "ownership" of God's Word, and Christian programmers should not restrict those who would like to read it.

Most importantly, we want the programs to honor Jesus through the work we do.
Jesus gave us our talents, and we are using them to honor Him.
